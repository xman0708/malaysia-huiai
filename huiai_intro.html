HuiAi 是一款集成大模型与低代码平台的智能体开发平台，面向政府、医疗、教育、金融等多个行业客户，帮助企业快速构建具备推理、问答、工作流和数据分析能力的 AI 应用。

HuiAi is a generative AI platform that enables businesses in healthcare, finance, government, and more to build industry-ready agents with reasoning, Q&A, workflow, and data analysis capabilities — all with low-code tools.
